Vehicular Vibration Simulator Platform

A project designed to test the performance of lead-acid batteries under simulated vehicular vibrations.

Learn More

Project Overview

In collaboration with the Advanced Batteries Center, this project aims to address the need for standardized vibration testing facilities for batteries used in electric vehicles (EVs). The simulator platform offers digital control over intensity and frequency, remote monitoring, and report generation through web and mobile applications.

Our goal is to develop a cost-effective prototype that meets IEC standards for battery testing, providing an affordable alternative to existing vibration equipment in the Philippines.
